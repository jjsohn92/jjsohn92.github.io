Hello, I am Jeongju Sohn :)

I am an assistant professor in School of Computer Science and Engineering at Kyungpook National University. Prior to KNU, I completed Ph.D. in School of Computing at KAIST and worked as a Postdoc in the Interdisciplinary Centre for Security, Reliability and Trust (SnT), University of Luxembourg. My research interests lie mainly in software engineering, specifically in software evolution, testing, and debugging. I am especially interested in leveraging complementary relationships between different activities in software maintenance throughout software evolution
